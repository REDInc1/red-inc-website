Our Services

Core Service: Act as middleman connecting customers to third-party negotiators who review initial insurance quotes, identify additional damages/repairs, and renegotiate for higher settlements (e.g., from $5,000 initial to $7,000 final).

Process:

  1. Identify prospects at body shops via manager relationships.
  2. Build rapport with customers, offering "free claim review" assistance.
  3. Discreetly engage negotiators to handle insurer communications.
  4. Customer receives enhanced payout; we take commission from the delta (additional amount).

Pricing: No upfront fees; commission-based (20-30% of recovered excess). Example: If we secure $2,000 extra, fee is $400-600.

Third-Party Partnerships: Contract with licensed insurance adjusters or negotiators on a per-case basis (pay them a flat fee or split commission).
